Layout

Top Bar

The top bar contains the title of the manual and Search, Table of Contents (TOC) and Settings charm icons. The three charm icons on the right of the top bar are Search, TOC and Settings.

The top bar has a fixed height of 50px and a horizontal linear gradient background with $dark-blue to $medium-blue color-stops. It is the only section of the CVA that will never have any custom styling.

On layouts smaller than 940px, or $topbar-breakpoint, the 'Settings' icon in the top bar is hidden and the settings navigation is combined with the TOC.

Table of Contents (TOC)

The Table of Contents, more commonly referred to as the TOC, acts as a navigation between pages of digital manuals. There are three customizable parts to the TOC: background, title and chapters.

Desktop/Console:

The TOC on layouts larger than $topbar-breakpoint is triggered by the TOC charm in the top bar and displays as an overlay on the right side of the screen.

  • Default
  • Custom

Tablet/Mobile:

The TOC on layouts smaller than $topbar-breakpoint is triggered by the "hamburger" menu icon and displays above settings menu in list-view

Content Area

The main content area of the CVA contains all of the manual content. The content area is wrapped in a div with a class of .content-main that spans the entire 12-column grid.

Backgrounds

Custom background colors, background-images, borders and border-images can be added by adding the following CSS to the theme stylesheet. Backgrounds can be added to the entire page by styling the body tag.

body { //define background for entire page background:url(images/example-bg.png); } .content-main { background: rgba(255,255,255,0.8); //define background image or color to content area border-width: 0 20px; //define background border image -moz-border-image:url('images/example-bg.png') 0 20 round; -webkit-border-image:url('images/example-bg.png') 0 20 round; -o-border-image:url('images/example-bg.png') 0 20 round; border-image:url('images/example-bg.png') 0 20 round; }
